Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 9 tbsp Milk powder
  • 3 ½ tbsp All purpose flour
  • ½ tbsp Semolina
  • ¾ tsp Baking soda
  • 2 tbsp Curd
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 tsp Ghee
  • ½ tsp Gulkand
  • 1 tsp Pistachios
  • 250 grams sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 2-3 Cardamom pods
  • 1 tbsp rose water

Recipe

  1. In a large bowl, add milk powder, all purpose flour, semolina, baking soda, curd, milk and mix everything well.
  2. Make a soft dough, divide the dough into equal portion sizes and roll it like a small ball.
  3. They must be smooth without cracks or lines. Keep them covered.
  4. Heat ghee in a kadai, drop a small ball of the dough to check the temperature of the ghee once it’s medium hot.
  5. Start frying the gulab jamun on medium flame make sure the ghee is not too hot or else the gulab jamun will brown without cooking.
  6. Fry the balls till light golden. Keep stirring gently so that it cooks from all the sides.
  7. In a saucepan, add water, sugar, cardamom pods, rose water and dissolve the sugar in water till it gets thicken on medium flame.
  8. Add them to the hot syrup. Allow them to rest at least 15-20 minutes.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2½ cup milk powder
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ cup ghee
  • 3 tbsp pistachios

Recipe

  1. firstly, in a bowl take 2½ cup milk powder, ¾ cup sugar and 1 cup milk.
  2. whisk and mix well until everything is well combined.
  3. transfer the mixture into large kadai. recommend using nonstick pan to prevent from sticking
  4. also add ¼ cup ghee and mix well on low flame.
  5. keep stirring on low flame until the mixture thickens.
  6. the mixture will hold the shape and starts to separate the pan after 10 minutes.
  7. transfer the burfi dough into the tray lined with baking paper.
  8. press gently, making sure its leveled up.
  9. top with few chopped pistachios and press gently.
  10. cover and refrigerate for 1 hour or until it sets completely.
  11. after 1 hour, unmould the burfi and cut with sharp knife.
  12. finally, milk burfi recipe tastes great for a week when stored in an airtight container.
